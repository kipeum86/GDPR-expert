## C-477/21 — Médecins du Monde

**Parties:** Médecins du monde - Dokters van de Wereld ASBL v Commissaire général aux réfugiés et aux apatrides

**Judgment Date:** 2023-12-14

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:984

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU addressed GDPR implications of processing biometric and health data in the asylum/refugee context. The Court confirmed that even in public interest contexts like migration management, processing of special category data must strictly comply with Art. 9(2) conditions and proportionality requirements, with particular attention to the vulnerable status of data subjects.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 9, Art. 6(1)(e)
