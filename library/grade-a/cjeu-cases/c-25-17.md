## C-25/17 — Jehovan todistajat (Jehovah's Witnesses)

**Parties:** Tietosuojavaltuutettu v Jehovan todistajat

**Judgment Date:** 2018-07-10

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2018:551

**Significance:** landmark

### Key Holding

The CJEU held that the Jehovah's Witnesses community acts as a data controller for notes collected by its members during door-to-door preaching, even without accessing the data itself. The household exemption does not apply. This significantly broadened the concept of 'controller' and confirmed that organised collection of personal data outside the purely personal sphere falls under data protection law.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 2, Art. 4(7), Art. 9
