## C-741/21 — juris GmbH

**Parties:** GP v juris GmbH

**Judgment Date:** 2023-04-20

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:270

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU addressed the right of access to personal data appearing in a legal database's published court decisions. It confirmed that the right of access under Art. 15 GDPR applies even where personal data appears in publicly available documents, though practical limitations on the scope of compliance may apply.
