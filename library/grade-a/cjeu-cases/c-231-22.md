## C-231/22 — Belgian State (Right of Access Scope)

**Parties:** Belgian State v Autorité de protection des données

**Judgment Date:** 2024-01-11

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2024:7

**Significance:** notable

### Key Holding

The CJEU clarified the distinction between the right of access under GDPR Art. 15 and general administrative transparency rights. The Court held that Art. 15 grants access specifically to personal data and information about processing activities, not a general right to obtain administrative documents, even if those documents contain the data subject's personal data.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 15, Art. 12
