## C-487/21 — Österreichische Datenschutzbehörde (CRIF)

**Parties:** Österreichische Datenschutzbehörde and CRIF GmbH

**Judgment Date:** 2023-10-05

**ECLI:** ECLI:EU:C:2023:739

**Significance:** important

### Key Holding

The CJEU interpreted the right to obtain a 'copy' of personal data under Art. 15(3) GDPR as requiring a faithful and intelligible reproduction of all personal data undergoing processing. This means providing the data in a comprehensive format, including where necessary extracts from documents or databases, not merely a summary. This significantly expanded the practical scope of access requests.

### Related GDPR Articles

Art. 15, Art. 12
