## Garante v Wind Tre — EUR 16.7 Million

**DPA:** Garante per la protezione dei dati personali (Italy)

**Target Entity:** Wind Tre S.p.A.

**Fine Amount:** EUR 16,729,600

**Decision Date:** 2020-07-09

**Significance:** important

### Summary

The Garante fined Wind Tre EUR 16.7M for systematic unsolicited marketing via SMS, email, phone calls, and automated calls without consent. Hundreds of complaints revealed users could not effectively withdraw consent or exercise their right to object. The Garante prohibited further processing of unlawfully obtained data and ordered implementation of technical measures to control business partners.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1), Art. 5(2), Art. 6(1)(a), Art. 12, Art. 24, Art. 25
