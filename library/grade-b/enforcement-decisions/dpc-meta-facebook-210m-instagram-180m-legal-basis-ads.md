## DPC v Meta (Facebook EUR 210M + Instagram EUR 180M) — EUR 390 Million (Legal Basis for Ads)

**DPA:** DPC (Ireland)

**Target Entity:** Meta Platforms Ireland Limited

**Fine Amount:** EUR 390,000,000 (EUR 210M Facebook + EUR 180M Instagram)

**Decision Date:** 2023-01-04

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

Note: This is the breakdown of the EUR 390M already in the existing collection, included here for completeness of the fine structure. The DPC found Meta could not rely on 'performance of a contract' (Art. 6(1)(b)) as the legal basis for processing personal data for personalized advertising on Facebook (EUR 210M) and Instagram (EUR 180M). The EDPB issued a binding decision requiring the DPC to find an additional violation of transparency obligations.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(a), Art. 6(1), Art. 12, Art. 13(1)(c)
