## ICO v Marriott International — GBP 18.4 Million

**DPA:** ICO (United Kingdom)

**Target Entity:** Marriott International Inc.

**Fine Amount:** GBP 18,400,000 (approx. EUR 20,400,000)

**Decision Date:** 2020-10-30

**Significance:** landmark

### Summary

The ICO fined Marriott GBP 18.4M (reduced from an initial GBP 99.2M notice of intent) for a data breach originating from a 2014 cyberattack on Starwood Hotels, discovered only in 2018 after Marriott's 2016 acquisition. Approximately 339 million guest records were exposed globally. The case established that acquiring companies inherit data protection obligations and must conduct adequate cybersecurity due diligence during M&A transactions.

### Violated Articles

Art. 5(1)(f), Art. 32
