## Not Legal Advice

Nothing in this repository — including legal opinions, compliance assessments, risk analyses, GDPR article interpretations, or any other output generated by the agent — constitutes legal advice. The outputs are **informational and assistive only**. Do not rely on them as a substitute for qualified legal counsel.

EU data protection law is complex, jurisdiction-specific, and rapidly evolving. National implementations of EU Directives (such as the ePrivacy Directive) vary significantly across Member States. Enforcement practices differ between DPAs. **Only a qualified legal professional familiar with your specific jurisdiction and circumstances can provide reliable legal guidance.**

## AI Limitations — Hallucinations and Errors

This project is powered by a large language model (Claude). LLMs can and do produce **hallucinations, factual errors, and incorrect legal analysis**. Outputs may:

- Misidentify or misinterpret GDPR provisions, EDPB guidelines, or CJEU holdings
- Fabricate article numbers, case citations, or enforcement decisions that do not exist
- Apply incorrect legal reasoning or miss critical exceptions and derogations
- Fail to account for recent legislative amendments (including the Digital Omnibus Package)
- Overlook national implementation differences for EU Directives

While this agent includes a built-in **fact-checking sub-agent** that verifies citations against the knowledge base, this verification is not infallible. **A qualified human must always review and verify every output** before any reliance or action.

## Knowledge Base Accuracy

The knowledge base was collected from official EU sources (Publications Office CELLAR API, EDPB website) and processed through automated pipelines (XHTML parsing, PDF-to-Markdown conversion). While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy:

- Automated parsing may introduce formatting errors or lose nuanced formatting (tables, footnotes)
- The knowledge base reflects a point-in-time snapshot and may not include the most recent amendments
- PDF-to-Markdown conversion of EDPB guidelines may produce imperfect results
- Cross-reference mappings are generated algorithmically and may be incomplete

## Data Security and Privacy

All processing runs **locally on your filesystem** — no external servers or databases are involved. However, the agent makes LLM API calls, which means **legal text and your queries are sent to the model provider** (Anthropic).

If you intend to use this for real legal work with confidential documents:

- **At minimum**, use a **commercial API key** (not a free-tier or consumer plan) so that standard commercial data handling terms apply.
- **Ideally**, use an **Enterprise plan with a Zero Data Retention (ZDR) agreement**. Note that ZDR is **not a complete guarantee** — your data still passes through the provider's infrastructure. Review Anthropic's official documentation on [ZDR and data handling](https://code.claude.com/docs/en/zero-data-retention) to determine whether it meets your organization's requirements.
- **Always** check with your organization's **security, compliance, and IT/DPO departments** before processing any confidential, privileged, or personally identifiable information through any AI tool.
- Consider the irony: **ensure your use of a GDPR compliance tool is itself GDPR-compliant.**
